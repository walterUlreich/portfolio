Book Club

Book Club is a full-stack application that I created for my final project at CodeCraft School of Technology. Once Book Club members log in to their group's page, they can keep track of books that they have read, vote for next month's book, and use the chat application to chat with other group members. Additional features are currently in the works!

healthHawk

healthHawk is a full-stack application that I contributed to as part of a group hackathon project at the CodeCraft School of Technology. healthHawk allows the user to create and log in to a personal profile, where they can keep track of what foods they have eaten and how many calories they have consumed throughout the day.
